frequently Asked questions

Is buying this NFT  Ticket Pass worth it ?

Everyone values time, tools, and information differently. We believe the value of holding this NFT Ticket  Pass will far outweigh the mint price, but it is up to you to decide if it's worth it to you. Our commitment is to underpromise and overdeliver.

Is there a Discord ?

No, we initially won't have a Discord. We will communicate through the Collector dashboard and email. The primary purpose of the NFT is to unlock software, and we think everyone is in enough Discords already.

What are the funds being used for ?

This is a software business and the funds will go to continuing to improve the software by paying for operations and employees. We pay for all these things in fiat, so funds will be regularly converted to fiat and moved to a custody account. Unfortunately, we'll also be sending a large chunk of it to the US Government because we're a legit software company that pays taxes.

Is there a roadmap ?

Yes, see above for currently planned releases.

Can i start using the benefits today ?

Today, the focus is on releasing the NFT (which, if you haven't done it, is a lot). The roadmap above has approximate dates of when benefits will roll out.

How long is this Ticket Pass avalid ?

Each pass unlocks benefits for the calendar year(s) that it's dated. This is the Founder's Edition, and covers both 2022 and 2030. The stated pass benefits are not guaranteed past the end of 2030
